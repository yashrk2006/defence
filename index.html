Problem Statement

Time-Intensive Monitoring

Defence forces use drones for continuous surveillance, generating hours of video daily. Manual monitoring is slow and inefficient.

Human Error & Fatigue

Large teams are required for surveillance. Human fatigue leads to errors, causing critical threats to go unnoticed.

Missed Threats

Critical security events like intrusions, suspicious vehicles, and abandoned objects often slip through undetected.

Need: An automated real-time AI system that detects threats instantly without human supervision
